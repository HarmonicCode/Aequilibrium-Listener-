Active Listening and Empathetic Counselling Services

A professional listener simply listens to you.


It is a chance for you to talk about anything for an hour; without being judged or offered an opinion. ​


Although this is not a substitute for clinical care, our experts are highly educated, non-judgemental, respectful, and honest. They create a positive and safe environment; ensuring complete privacy.


Aequilibriium is an endeavour of providing Listening & Counselling Services for Integrated Wellness, Comprehensive Wellbeing and Holistic Healing of an Individual.

Do you just need to be heard anonymously and unencumbered, and not necessarily guided or counselled

Don't worry, professional listeners are here to listen ...

It is a basic human need to be heard.

We hide our feelings and frustrations as those around us do not listen when we speak. A professional listener helps by being on the receiving end of the conversation, ensuring that your fundamental needs are met.

Professional listening is not coaching, counseling or therapy. It is an opportunity to speak with someone who will listen openly, empathize with what you're saying, and embrace the topic, subject, or area you wish to discuss.

Listeners may react, but only if you want them to.

About

Aequilibriium by Rana Vikram Singh is an endeavour of providing Active Listening & Empathetic Counselling Services for Integrated Wellness, Comprehensive Wellbeing and Holistic Healing.


Integrated Wellness is a unified way that combines various practices and disciplines to promote overall well-being and use different methods and strategies to create a balanced and effective wellness.


Comprehensive Wellbeing refers to a state of overall health and vitality that encompasses multiple dimensions of a person's life. It goes beyond physical health to include mental, emotional, social, and spiritual aspects. Comprehensive wellbeing implies a sense of balance, fulfillment, and flourishing in all areas of life, leading to a high quality of life and a sense of overall satisfaction and contentment.


Holistic Healing is an approach to wellness that considers the whole person – mind, body, and spirit, to emphasize the interconnectedness of these aspects and seeks to promote balance and harmony as it aims to address the root causes of ailments and promote balance and harmony across all aspects of an individual's life.


Why Taking Helps

  • It is an alternative way to heal and relax. Talking to someone when you're having a hard time is one of the most effective things you can do to feel better. Just talking about it can help you get the clarity and relief you deserve.​

  • Seeking support can sensibly share the weight of immediate stress (Reid et al., 2009). According to experts, talking can reduce stress and make your worries feel less overwhelming.

  • Sometimes, all you want is for someone to listen rather than try to fix your concerns.

  • Talking to someone can make you feel less isolated, like you're not experiencing things alone.

  • If you talk about a problem, you can begin to break it down into smaller parts, which will help you feel less overwhelmed and start to see it as more manageable.

  • Having someone to listen can be transformative and help priorities emerge loud and clear.
